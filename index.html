    About us

    Like every good story, ours starts with a mother, Rosa Galindo, and her son, Ricardo Galindo, who both have a passion for maintaining a family business and providing honest and excellent service to their customers. Rosa started in the landscaping business in 1972 working with landscaping engineers and architects in San Mateo, CA. She taught her sons along the way and in 1980 started her own business, Rosa's Garden and Landscape Service, with her son Ricardo providing the East Bay with expert landscape and garden services.


    As with all things, change is necessary to expand and provide even better services. In 2013, Ricardo received his degrees in Landscape Architecture and Design Architect from West Valley College. The business now provides these services to both residential and commercial businesses. As a locally-owned family-run business, we provide a host of services to meet your needs with an unsurpassed level of detail and flexibility. We provide weekly, bi-weeky, and monthly services to accommodate your schedule for both residential and commercial businesses.
